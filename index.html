Raising Awareness about Internet Addiction and Physical Health

Welcome to our website dedicated to understanding and addressing internet addiction among Grade 11 students at San Felipe Neri Catholic School. The internet has become an integral part of our daily lives, offering many benefits and conveniences. However, excessive internet use can lead to negative consequences, including physical health problems. This website aims to raise awareness about the impact of internet addiction on physical health and to provide effective solutions to minimize its negative effects. Through our research and insights, we hope to empower young people to develop healthy internet usage habits and to prevent the negative consequences of excessive internet use. We invite you to explore our website and join us in creating a healthier and safer online community for young people.

Mission

To raise awareness about the impact of internet addiction on the physical health of young people, and to empower youth to make informed choices about their internet usage habits. We are committed to promoting healthy internet behaviors and providing resources and support to those struggling with internet addiction. Through our advocacy and education efforts, we aim to create a healthier and safer online community for young people.

Vision

To create a world where young people can enjoy the benefits of the internet without sacrificing their physical and mental health. We believe that with increased awareness and education, young people can develop healthy internet habits that will serve them well throughout their lives. We envision a future where the internet is a tool for growth, learning, and connection, rather than a source of addiction and harm.

Why Internet Addiction is a Growing Concern

The internet has transformed the way we live, work, and connect with one another. However, excessive use of the internet can lead to a range of negative consequences, including addiction, isolation, and physical health problems. Internet addiction is a growing concern among young people, with many spending hours each day online, often at the expense of other activities such as exercise, socializing, and sleep. As more young people become addicted to the internet, it is essential to raise awareness of the negative consequences and provide effective solutions for managing internet use.
